title: Campaign Survey Distribution
description: Send a survey to the members of a Salesforce Campaign using unique, single-use invitation links, and track responses back on the Campaign.

Campaign Survey Distribution lets you send an AutoSurvey survey to the members of a Salesforce **Campaign**. Each eligible member gets a **unique, single-use invitation link**, and their progress (started, completed, abandoned) rolls back up to the Campaign and its members.

The whole experience lives on a panel on the Campaign record page inside the **AutoSurvey** app.

## Before you begin

| Requirement | Why |
| --- | --- |
| **AutoSurvey Setup Home is healthy** | The panel depends on a working org. Open the **Setup** tab and confirm required checks pass first. See the [Setup Home Guide](/admin/setup). |
| **Admin permissions** | You need `ASVY_Deploy_Admin` (or the `ASVY_Full_Admin` group). The panel is hidden from guest users and non-admins. |
| **A deployed survey form** | The Campaign is linked to a **Form**, which belongs to a survey **Suite**. Create and deploy a survey first. |
| **(Public surveys) An Experience Cloud site** | To collect responses from unauthenticated respondents, publish the survey flow on an Experience page and assign `AutoSurvey_Guest_Access` to the site guest user. See [Setup Home → Public Survey Access](/admin/setup). |

## Open the panel

1. Switch to the **AutoSurvey** app (App Launcher → AutoSurvey).
2. Click the **Campaigns** tab and open (or create) a Campaign.
3. The **AutoSurvey Campaign Distribution** panel appears on the record page.

> The custom Campaign record page is applied **only inside the AutoSurvey app**. Your organization's default Campaign page in other apps is not changed.

Every field on the panel has an inline help icon (the small circled **i**). Hover or click it for a quick explanation.

## Step 1 — Configure delivery

| Field | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Form** | The survey form sent to this campaign's members. Each eligible member receives a unique, single-use link to this form. Changing the form after links exist starts a new survey for members who have not completed the old one. |
| **Delivery Enabled** | Turn **on** to allow this campaign to generate invitation links and deliver surveys. Turn **off** to pause delivery without losing existing links or responses. |
| **Save Settings** | Saves the selected Form and Delivery Enabled values to the Campaign record. |

After you pick a form and click **Save Settings**, the **Suite** for that form is shown under Distribution status.

## Distribution status

A live snapshot of this campaign's survey progress. Counts refresh after links are generated and after the nightly sync.

| Metric | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Suite** | The survey suite that contains the selected form. |
| **Members** | Total campaign members. Members without a Contact or Lead, or who have opted out of email, are skipped when generating links. |
| **Tokens** | Members who already have an invitation link generated for the selected form. |
| **Started** | Members who have opened their invitation and begun the survey. |
| **In Progress** | Members with a saved but not-yet-completed submission. |
| **Completed** | Members who finished the survey. |
| **Abandoned** | Drafts whose invitation expired before completion (partial answers are kept). |

## Step 2 — Build the survey link

This section assembles a merge-ready link for public (unauthenticated) distribution.

| Field | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Experience Site Domain** | The public Experience Cloud site that hosts your survey page. Sites marked **Ready** have the `AutoSurvey_Guest_Access` permission assigned to their guest user. |
| **Survey Page Path** | The path to the Experience page that hosts the Survey Runner LWC, for example `/survey`. Combined with the site domain to build the full link. |
| **Merge-ready URL Template** | A copy-ready link for your email or merge tool. The `{!Survey_Invitation__c.Invite_Token__c}` placeholder is replaced with each member's unique token at send time. |

> The template uses your email platform's merge syntax. Paste it into the campaign email so each recipient receives their own tokenized link.

## Generate invitation links

Click **Generate Links** to create a unique invitation for each eligible campaign member.

What happens:

- One `Survey_Invitation__c` is created per eligible member, each with its own single-use token and a 14‑day expiration.
- Members are **skipped** when they:
  - Have **no Contact or Lead** identity on the campaign member.
  - Have **opted out of email** (`HasOptedOutOfEmail`).
  - Already have an **active invitation for the same form from another campaign** (duplicate suppression).
- Members who already have a link for this form are **reused**, never re-tokenized — existing links keep working.

After generation, a summary shows how many invitations were **created, reused, suppressed,** and **opt-out skipped**.

You can also click **Copy Member Links** to copy a tab-separated export
(`Member Name`, `Email`, `Survey Status`, `Survey URL`) for the campaign.

### How responses are attributed

When a respondent opens their link and clicks **Start**, AutoSurvey:

1. Resolves the token to the campaign member, campaign, and the form recorded on the invitation (the link cannot be swapped to a different form).
2. Creates or resumes a single submission and links it to the invitation.
3. Stamps the first-opened time and tracks status as **In Progress → Completed**.

A nightly sync rolls each member's status, completion time, and latest invitation up to the **Campaign Member**, and updates the Campaign's **Total Completed**, **Completion Rate**, and **Last Sync** rollups.

## Validate the survey URL

Click **Validate URL** once after publishing your Experience page. AutoSurvey opens the survey link with a one-time validation code and confirms the page loads the **correct flow**, then marks the form's URL as **validated** with a timestamp. This catches broken paths, wrong sites, or unpublished pages before you send.

## Email delivery in Phase 1

Phase 1 uses native Salesforce sending or export workflows. AutoSurvey does not
send campaign emails directly in this phase.

Recommended flow:

1. Generate links from the campaign panel.
2. Use a Campaign Member email template with `{!CampaignMember.Survey_Link__c}`.
3. Send via List Email from the Campaign.

Fallback flow:

1. Copy/export member links (`Survey Link`) from the panel/report.
2. Send through your external email platform.

Important caveats:

- Prefer **Classic** templates for reliable Campaign Member merge behavior.
- List Email send limits and org deliverability policies still apply.
- If a member has blank `Survey_Link__c`, the merged email can contain no URL.

For Apex-based test sends, confirm **Setup -> Deliverability** is set to
**All email**. In scratch/dev orgs, **System email only** often causes sends to
"succeed" in code but never deliver to inboxes.

## Send survey emails (Phase 3)

Phase 3 adds direct bulk send from the Campaign Distribution panel.

Prerequisites:

- Campaign has a saved **Form**
- **Delivery Enabled** is on
- Invitation links were generated
- Org deliverability is **All email**

Workflow:

1. Review the audience summary (`eligible`, `already sent`, `completed`,
   `opted out`, `no identity`, `suppressed`, `missing email`, `need links`).
2. Enter subject + body.
3. Include `%%LINK%%` in the body where each member-specific survey URL should
   be inserted (AutoSurvey appends the link if the placeholder is missing).
4. Pick an Organization-Wide From address (optional).
5. Click **Send Emails**.

Delivery visibility:

- The panel includes a **Delivery log** table (invitation-level) with:
  - member/email
  - invitation status
  - sent timestamp
  - send count
  - first opened timestamp
  - expiration timestamp
  - last send error
- Use filter chips for **All / Sent / Not sent / Errors / Completed**.
- Row actions:
  - **Resend invitation**
  - **Copy link**
  - **Open invitation record**
- Use **Download Delivery Log (CSV)** for external review.

Default send policy:

- Already-sent invitations are skipped by default.
- Turn on **Resend to all eligible** to include already-sent invitations.
- If one or more members are in **need links**, Send is blocked to prevent a
  partial batch. Click **Generate links for missing members** first, then send.
- You can resend only specific invitations with **Resend selected (N)**, or use
  the row-level **Resend invitation** action.

Tracking fields:

- `Survey_Invitation__c.Sent_At__c`
- `Survey_Invitation__c.Send_Count__c`
- `Survey_Invitation__c.Last_Send_Error__c`
- `Campaign.Total_Sent__c`
- `Campaign.Last_Send_At__c`

Important caveats:

- Salesforce daily/org email limits still apply.
- Opted-out members are excluded from send eligibility.
- Missing `Survey_URL__c` means no send target for that member until links are generated.
- Targeted resend still skips invitations that are completed, opted out,
  expired, missing email, or missing URL (skip reasons are written to
  `Survey_Invitation__c.Last_Send_Error__c`).

## Privacy and retention

- Invitation tokens are single-use and expire (default 14 days).
- A scheduled job marks expired, incomplete drafts as **Abandoned** while preserving partial answers.
- Resume secrets are stored hashed at rest.

For the full data-handling model, see [Security and Privacy](/admin/security-and-privacy).

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause / fix |
| --- | --- |
| **"Campaign must be linked to a form…"** | Select a **Form** and click **Save Settings** before generating links. |
| **Many members suppressed** | Members already have an active invitation for the same form from another campaign, or have opted out of email. Review the generation summary. |
| **No Experience sites listed / all "Not configured"** | The site must be **Active** (publish and activate it in Experience setup — use the **Open Experience setup** link by the Experience Site Domain field), and the guest user must have `AutoSurvey_Guest_Access` assigned. Newly created sites are inactive until you activate them. See [Setup Home → Public Survey Access](/admin/setup). |
| **Validate URL fails** | Confirm the Experience page is published, the path is correct, and the page runs the matching survey flow. |
| **Panel not visible on the Campaign** | Confirm you are in the **AutoSurvey** app and have admin permissions (`ASVY_Deploy_Admin`). |
| **Counts look stale** | Rollups refresh after generation and on the nightly sync; allow a sync cycle to elapse. |
